# Customer Segmentation Using Machine Learning

## Overview

Customer segmentation is an important data analytics technique used to classify customers into distinct groups based on their behavior, purchasing patterns, and demographic characteristics. Understanding these customer groups enables businesses to develop targeted marketing strategies, improve customer engagement, and enhance overall business performance.

This project applies machine learning-based clustering techniques to analyze customer data and identify meaningful customer segments. By grouping customers with similar characteristics, organizations can make data-driven decisions and provide personalized services that better meet customer needs.

---

## Problem Statement

Modern businesses generate large volumes of customer data through transactions, interactions, and marketing activities. However, extracting meaningful insights from this data can be challenging. Treating all customers as a single group often leads to inefficient marketing efforts and reduced customer satisfaction.

The goal of this project is to analyze customer information and automatically identify customer segments using machine learning techniques. These segments can then be used to support targeted marketing campaigns and strategic business decisions.

---

## Objectives

* Analyze customer demographics and behavioral patterns.
* Identify similarities and differences among customers.
* Segment customers into meaningful groups using clustering algorithms.
* Visualize customer segments for easier interpretation.
* Generate actionable business insights from the segmented data.
* Support personalized marketing and customer relationship strategies.

## Methodology

### 1. Data Collection

Customer data is collected and imported for analysis. The dataset serves as the foundation for identifying customer groups.

### 2. Data Preprocessing

The collected data undergoes preprocessing to improve quality and consistency.

Tasks performed include:

* Handling missing values
* Removing duplicate records
* Data cleaning
* Feature selection
* Data normalization and scaling

### 3. Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

Exploratory Data Analysis is performed to understand the structure and characteristics of the dataset.

Analysis includes:

* Distribution of customer attributes
* Income and spending patterns
* Correlation analysis
* Identification of trends and outliers

### 4. Feature Engineering

Relevant features are selected and prepared to improve clustering performance and segmentation accuracy.

### 5. Customer Segmentation

Machine learning clustering techniques are applied to group customers based on similarities in their characteristics and purchasing behavior.

### 6. Data Visualization

Various visualization methods are used to represent customer clusters and interpret segmentation results effectively.

### 7. Insight Generation

Business insights are derived from each customer segment to assist in marketing strategy development and decision-making.
